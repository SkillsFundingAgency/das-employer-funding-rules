The latest rules (2018 to 2019)

There are different funding rules for different apprenticeship start dates. You must follow the funding rules that apply to each apprentice.

2017 to 2018 rules

The 2017 to 2018 rules apply to apprenticeships that started between 1 August 2017 and 31 July 2018. You can view the apprenticeship funding rules for:
